Plutonium Pit Production at Savannah River Site (SRS); Aiken, SC

The DOE National Nuclear Security Administration (NNSA) announced its intent to prepare an EIS that evaluates the potential environmental impacts of alternatives for plutonium pit production at the Savannah River Site (SRS) in South Carolina. The 2018 Nuclear Posture Review announced that the United States will pursue initiatives to ensure the necessary capability, capacity, and responsiveness of the nuclear weapons infrastructure and the needed skill of the workforce, including providing the enduring capability and capacity to produce no fewer than 80 plutonium pits per year by 2030.

For more information, see Plutonium Pit Production at Savannah River Site (SRS).
